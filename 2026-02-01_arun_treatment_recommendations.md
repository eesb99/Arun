### Calvert Formula
```
Carboplatin Dose (mg) = Target AUC x (GFR + 25)

Standard AUC targets:
- Single agent: AUC 5-6
- Combination: AUC 4-5
- Compromised renal: AUC 4
```

### Recommendation: Immunotherapy Approach
**For Arun (EGFR-mutant + cardiac/renal compromise):**

1. **Expected ICI efficacy:** ~10-20% (vs 30-40% in wild-type)
2. **Risk assessment:**
   - Pre-existing cardiac dysfunction increases myocarditis risk
   - Need baseline ECG, troponin, echocardiogram
3. **Decision:**
   - **Prefer TKI or chemo alone** over chemo-IO combination
   - If ICI considered, use single-agent (not dual checkpoint blockade)
   - Intensive cardiac monitoring required

## 7. Treatment Decision Matrix

### Given Arun's Constraints (Heart Failure + Renal + Cytopenias)

**Tier 1: Lowest Toxicity (PREFERRED)**
| Option | Why | Requirement |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| **Continue/Switch EGFR-TKI** | Minimal myelosuppression, oral | Know mutation type |
| Lazertinib | Cardio-safer than osimertinib | T790M or prior osimertinib failure |

**Tier 2: Moderate Toxicity**
| Option | Why | Requirement |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Single-agent carboplatin | Cardiac safe, renal adjustable | GFR calculation |
| Single-agent gemcitabine | Cardiac safe | Monitor platelets |

**Tier 3: Higher Toxicity**
| Option | Why | Requirement |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Carbo + pemetrexed | Standard doublet | CrCl >45, adequate counts |
| Carbo + gemcitabine | Alternative doublet | PLT >100K |

**NOT RECOMMENDED**
- Cisplatin (renal)
- Bevacizumab (cardiac)
- Dual ICI (cardiac risk + low efficacy in EGFR+)
- Anthracyclines (cardiac)
